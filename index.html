Forecasting Gold Return

The aim of this report is to predict the monthly return of gold in 12 months of 2018 base on the LBMA's monthly gold prices from January 2002 to December 2018 by choosing an adequate ARIMA model.

The monthly evening close price from January 2002 to December 2018 (204 observed prices) is used for the analysis. The data is obtained from www.quandl.com.

All the forecast values fall in the 95% forecast limit.

Money Deposits

Bank deposits are money placed into banking institutions such as savings accounts, checking accounts, and money market accounts by the account holder (customer). Bank deposits are essential because they stimulate economic growth.

As of 2019, JPMorgan Chase has the most deposits in the US, with $1.139 trillion. However, Chase still not dominate the deposits market in Ohio. One way to increase the number of deposits is to open more branches so people in every area can access the bank easier.

Prediction for money deposits at locations that do not have Chase bank is built by using the fitted spatial-temporal covariance model and spatial-temporal universal kriging. The model is adequate since there is no pattern in the residual.

Forecasting Salesforce (CRM) Stock

Salesforce.com, Inc. (CRM) is a cloud-based software company headquartered in San Francisco, California. State-to-State Migration Flows in 10 years

Where did people go and come from in the last 10 years? Each year, there is 18,000 to 600,000 people move from one state to another state. I cleaned and merged the raw data in Python. I also created a dashboard allowing to explore the data. It allows to understand the different in out-migration and in-migration between each state and how the migration flows change over year.

The dataset for this project originates from the US Census Bureau. The dataset contains the State-to-State migration flows for 10 years from 2010 to 2019.
