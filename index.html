Forecasting Gold Return

The goal of this report is to predict the monthly return of gold for a period of 12 months of 2018 based on the LBMA's monthly gold prices between January 2002 and December 2017 using an adequate ARIMA model.

The monthly evening close price from January 2002 to December 2018 (204 observed prices) is used for the analysis. The data is obtained from www.quandl.com.

All the forecast values fall in the 95% forecast limit.

Money Deposits

Bank deposits are money that a person deposits into a bank account such as a savings account or a checking account, or an account in a money market fund. Deposits of this type are essential because they boost economic growth.

As of 2019, JPMorgan Chase is the largest depositor in the US, with $1.139 trillion. However, JPMorgan Chase did not dominate the deposits market in Ohio. A way to increase deposits is to open more branches so people in every area can easily access the bank.

Predictions for deposits at locations without Chase banks are developed by fitting a spatial-temporal covariance model and using spatial-temporal universal kriging. The model is sufficient since no pattern appears in the residuals.

Forecasting Salesforce (CRM) Stock

Salesforce.com, Inc., a cloud-based software company headquartered in San Francisco, California, provides customer relationship management (CRM) service and technology to manage relationships and interactions with customers. More than 150,000 companies use Salesforce for their CRM needs. Salesforce reported earnings of $127 million for 2018.

The purpose of this report is to predict the daily stock price of CRM for the last 20 days of 2019 by choosing an appropriate ARIMA model and GARCH model based on the CRM daily stock price from January 2016 to December 2019.
